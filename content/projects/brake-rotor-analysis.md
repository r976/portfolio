## Objective

Assess brake rotor material and geometry choices for a Baja SAE vehicle under braking loads and elevated temperature conditions.

## Method

Built the rotor geometry in Siemens NX and evaluated thermal and structural behavior using ANSYS Mechanical. The study compared candidate materials, investigated temperature reduction targets, and established a repeatable workflow for future design updates.

## Key Learning

The project strengthened practical understanding of boundary-condition selection, material-property sensitivity, mesh refinement, and the importance of validating simulation assumptions against real vehicle operating conditions.
