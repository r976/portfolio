## Objective

Convert high-speed boiling imagery into measurable bubble geometry for heat-transfer research.

## Approach

Combined object detection and segmentation models with custom Python geometry tools. The pipeline extracted bubble masks, generated rotational reconstructions, exported meshes, and recorded geometric metadata for later analysis.

## Deliverables

- Automated and manual review workflows
- OBJ, XYZ, JSONL, and CSV exports
- Bubble area, centroid, and estimated volume
- Watertight mesh generation and visualization
