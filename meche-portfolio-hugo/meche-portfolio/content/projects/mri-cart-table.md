## Objective

Develop a detailed, manufacturable table assembly from incomplete legacy sketches while preserving compatibility with existing veterinary equipment and adapting the platform for a Siemens MAGNETOM Free.Max MRI installation.

## Engineering Work

- Built the table as a fully constrained Fusion 360 assembly.
- Created new components and production drawings while updating legacy parts to reflect current fabrication practices.
- Designed a side-mounted support attachment intended to position portions of a horse within the MRI bore.
- Coordinated with fabricators to verify dimensions, assembly sequence, and practical manufacturing constraints.
- Performed preliminary structural analysis to identify areas requiring further review.

## Outcome

The project established a controlled digital assembly that can support design revisions, manufacturing documentation, and future validation. Replace this section with quantified results once testing and fabrication are complete.
